# learn-to-play / Duduk (A)

Starter songbook for the **A duduk** (Armenian double-reed) from the [`duduk`](https://github.com/tonykoop/duduk) build packet. Generated by the [`sheet-music`](https://github.com/tonykoop/sheet-music) skill.

The duduk is a double-reed instrument with a warm, vocal tone. Its character comes from
**glides** (slow portamento between notes) and **sustained drone-like phrases**.

Range: **G3 – C5** (approximately). Chromatic. The A duduk's fundamental is A3.

## Notes on duduk notation
Grace notes in these ABC files indicate **glides** — slow portamento slides, not quick
ornamental grace notes. When you see `{X}Y`, slide slowly from X into Y over about half a beat.
