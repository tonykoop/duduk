# Ov Sirun Sirun (Ով Սիրուն Սիրուն / Oh Beautiful)

**Tradition:** Trad Armenian folk song (pre-1900)
**Public domain:** Yes — traditional, pre-1929 (US)
**Arrangement:** A natural minor for A duduk. Range A3–A4.

## Practice tip
A gentle 3/4 — feel it as a slow waltz. The `{F}E` and `{B}A` grace notes are
descending glides: approach from above and fall into the note. Keep them slow and smooth.
