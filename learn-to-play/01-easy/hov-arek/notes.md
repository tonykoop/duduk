# Hov Arek (Ով Արեք / Hover My Dove)

**Tradition:** Trad Armenian folk song (pre-1900)
**Public domain:** Yes — traditional, pre-1929 (US)
**Arrangement:** D dorian for A duduk. Range A3–F4.

## Practice tip
The opening gesture `{A,}D` (glide from A3 up to D4) sets the tone for the whole tune.
Practice that glide until it's smooth and slow. The rest follows naturally.
