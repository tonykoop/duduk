# Grung (Կռունկ / The Crane)

**Tradition:** Trad Armenian; one of the oldest and most beloved duduk songs
**Public domain:** Yes — traditional folk song, pre-1900
**Arrangement:** A minor/Phrygian for A duduk. Range A3–E4.

## Context
*Grung* means "crane" in Armenian. The duduk's vocal, sighing tone is considered the
closest any instrument comes to the sound of longing and exile in Armenian culture.
This melody has been recorded by Djivan Gasparyan and is the quintessential duduk tune.

## Glide notation
`{B,}A,` = begin slightly above A3 and slide down into it. This is a smooth portamento,
not a quick grace note. Take about half a beat for each glide.

## Practice tip
Play it at ♩=50 or slower. This tune is not about speed — it's about breath and resonance.
Each phrase should sound like a human voice.
