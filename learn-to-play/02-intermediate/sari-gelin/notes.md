# Sari Gelin (Sarı Gəlin / Sarig Yar — Fair-Haired Bride)

**Tradition:** Trad Armenian/Azerbaijani folk song; origin disputed, pre-1900
**Public domain:** Yes — traditional folk melody, pre-1929 (US)
**Arrangement:** A natural minor/Phrygian for A duduk. Range A3–E4.

## Context
A deeply emotional song — associated with longing, loss, and the Caucasian landscape.
Played by duduk players across the region. The Phrygian b2 (Bb in A Phrygian) gives
it its characteristic aching quality.

## Practice tip
Every phrase in this tune is a glide. The `{X}Y` markings are guideposts, but in
practice you should be gliding almost continuously — let the pitch drift expressively.
