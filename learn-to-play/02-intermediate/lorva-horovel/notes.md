# Lorva Horovel (Plowing Song)

**Tradition:** Trad Armenian labor song (pre-1900)
**Public domain:** Yes — traditional, pre-1929 (US)
**Arrangement:** A Phrygian, 7/8 meter (3+2+2) for A duduk.

## Context
Work songs in Armenian tradition often use asymmetric meters that match the rhythm of
physical labour. The 7/8 here is 3+2+2: ONE-two-three, ONE-two, ONE-two.

## Practice tip
Count the 7/8 as 3-2-2 out loud before playing. The duduk's drone quality
suits repetitive patterns — once you have the rhythm, the melody almost plays itself.
