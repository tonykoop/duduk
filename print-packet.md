# Duduk Family - Printable Shop Packet

**Readiness:** L2 private draft. Physical build, measured validation, and cultural review are pending.

## Critical Sequence

1. Source several reeds before final body tuning.
2. Bore and turn the A3-root body.
3. Fit the reed socket without air leaks.
4. Tune body length against the selected reed.
5. Drill and tune holes empirically.
6. Complete cultural review before public release.
